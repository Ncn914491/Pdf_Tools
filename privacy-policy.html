Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

TL;DR: PDF Toolkit is a completely offline app. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your files never leave your device.

1. Introduction

PDF Toolkit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data. PDF Toolkit is designed to work completely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account creation or login
  • Upload your files to any server

3. How Your Files Are Processed

All PDF and image processing happens locally on your device:

  • Files are processed using on-device libraries (PdfBox-Android, ML Kit)
  • Temporary files are stored in your device's cache and automatically cleaned up
  • Output files are saved to your device's storage (Downloads or custom location)
  • No files are ever transmitted over the internet

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read PDF/image files and save processed files
  • Camera (Optional): For the "Scan to PDF" feature only

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and never for data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

PDF Toolkit does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage services

6. Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Recent Files List: URI references to files you've opened (for quick access)
  • App Settings: Your preferences (compression quality, default format, etc.)
  • Temporary Cache: Temporary files created during processing (automatically cleaned)

You can clear this data at any time through the app's Settings or by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

PDF Toolkit does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: developerncn29@gmail.com

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear recent files history in the app's Files tab
  • Clear cache in the app's Settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
